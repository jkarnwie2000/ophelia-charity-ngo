our shop

Shop with us for the best in gifts and souvenirs for your family, friends, or loved ones. Bring joy and appreciation to that special person in your life with any selections from the specially crafted gifts and souvenirs items in our online store. When you make a purchase, 50 percent of the proceeds go toward helping the underprivileged school children that we support. Our silky cotton caps and T-shirts and our beautifully crafted keychains give comfort, beauty, and convenience. Shop with us now to show that special someone how much you love and appreciate them.
