Your donation will help give a child a brighter future

Our team of friendly volunteers are here to assist you with your donation. You can give at anytime wherever you are. Give online by clicking DONATE, or contact us with any questions.

We are trustworthy

We are a legally registered not-for-profit organization in the Republic of Liberia, and our accounts are independently audited by a reputable accountancy firm. Our financial records are also available for review upon your request.

Be a generous giver

When you give to this cause, many underprivileged children are helped with school fees, school supplies, and bookbags. Your every dollar goes to bless a child who could not have gotten an education otherwise. Your giving makes a big difference in the life of a less fortunate child.

We have the best volunteer support team

Our helpful customer support team will answer your donation-related and other questions, clarify our mission and vision, and take your online purchase payments.

See where your money goes

60 percent of your donation goes toward school fees, 20 percent toward school supplies, 10 percent toward bookbags, and 10 percent toward administrative costs. Speak to one of our volunteers for more information about where your money goes.

recent events

Quite recently, we visited many schools in the poorest parts of Liberia to assess the needs of the underpreviliged children and ways that we could help them, and the three main issues that came up were the needs for school fees, school supplies, and bookbags. Many school children between ages 5 and 16 become dropouts because they are either orphaned with no support, or their parents are too poor to meet their educational needs. And most of these children are talented and need just a little push to complete their education and make impacts on our world. Therefore, your giving is a blessing that could go a long way to help them succeed. Thanks for your generous support.
