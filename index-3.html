WHERE YOUR MONEY GOES

Every dollar you give will help pay tuition fees and buy school supplies and bookbags for the underprivileged school children. Many children who could have become dropouts due to poverty could be blessed by your giving. Thanks for being a generous supporter of this great cause.

  • 60% money goes to school fees.
  • 20% money goes to school supplies.
  • 10% money goes to bookbags.
  • 10% money goes to administrative costs.
